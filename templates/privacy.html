Privacy Policy

Career Quest Privacy Policy

Career Quest is designed to help players explore careers, track progress, earn achievements, and discover career interests.

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Information Career Quest Stores

Career Quest may store basic app progress information so the app can work properly for each player.

  • Player name
  • XP, level, and streak progress
  • Completed career simulations
  • Quiz scores and career match results
  • Achievements earned
  • Saved careers
  • Daily challenge progress

How This Information Is Used

This information is used only to power Career Quest features.

  • Showing your dashboard and profile progress
  • Saving XP, levels, streaks, and achievements
  • Displaying career history and saved careers
  • Improving career recommendations inside the app
  • Showing leaderboard and app activity totals

Information Career Quest Does Not Collect

  • No passwords
  • No payment information
  • No precise location data
  • No contacts
  • No health data
  • No advertising tracking data
  • No third-party ad tracking

Data Sharing

Career Quest does not sell player data. Career Quest does not use player data for advertising tracking.

If third-party services are added in the future, this policy should be updated to explain what those services collect and how they use data.

Data Storage

Career Quest stores progress data in its app database so player progress can be saved between sessions.

Deleting Or Resetting Data

Players can switch profiles inside the app. To request deletion of stored player progress, contact support at careerquestappsupport@gmail.com
