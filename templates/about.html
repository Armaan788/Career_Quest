About

Career Quest

Career Quest is an interactive career exploration app that helps players discover possible futures through simulations, quizzes, XP, achievements, and personalized career matching.

Find My Career Explore Careers
Version 1.0

Created by Armaan Sharma

Purpose

Built For Career Exploration

Career Quest makes career discovery feel active instead of boring. Players can try different career paths, see how their choices affect their scores, save careers they like, and build a profile over time.

Career Simulations

Try realistic career scenarios and see how your decisions connect to skills like leadership, communication, technical thinking, and problem solving.

Personal Progress

Earn XP, build streaks, unlock achievements, save careers, and track your journey from your dashboard.

Career Matching

Answer career match questions to find careers that fit your interests, strengths, and preferred work style.

Creator

Created By Armaan Sharma

Career Quest was built as a learning project and product idea to help people explore careers in a more interactive way.
